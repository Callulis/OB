Steven Rubin

Advisor

Steven Rubin previously was the President and Chief Operating Officer at Bio Science Contract Production Corp. Steven oversaw the construction of a 54,000 square foot biologics manufacturing facility. In addition, he was responsible for all the financial and operational aspects of the company. As COO, he raised over $40 million in capital. $16.5 Million in special funding appropriated by the state legislature. An additional $750,000 was raised from the EDA.

The remainder was raised from private investors and banks. He oversaw the reporting to several government agencies including the State of Maryland, The EDA, and the FDA. He negotiated the sale of Bio Science to Cambrex for $130 million in 2001. At Cambrex, he was a Senior Vice President of Operations, responsible for all the operational aspects of the Bio Business Unit. He was on the acquisitions team, and oversaw the integration of an additional biologics manufacturing plant. He left Cambrex in 2002 to become President and CEO of Lee’s Ice Cream.

He oversaw the construction of a 20,000 square foot ice cream manufacturing plant. While at Lee’s, Steven led the financial, administrative, strategic and operational performance for this start-up entity. Under Steven’s leadership, Lee’s became a national known brand, with ice cream in stores from coast to coast. Realizing the financial requirements of being a nationwide company competing with multi-billion dollar corporations, Steven restructured the Lee’s to a Mid-Atlantic company. In 2008 Lee’s purchased a small local brand increasing it’s local presence. He successfully sold both brands in January of 2009.
